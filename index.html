Background

Christopher Comstock (Born May 19, 1992) is professionally known as Marshmello, is an American electronic music producer and dj. He first gained international recognition by releasing remixes of songs.His debut album, Joytime was released in Jauary 2016 consisting of its lead and Marshmello's debut career single "Keep t Mello" He is best known for his songs "Silence", "Wolves" (With Selena Gomez), "Happier", and "Friends". Marshmello's second song in 2016, a platinum-certified single titled "Alone" was released in May via the Canadian record "Monster cat"
